Admissions 2025

Start Your Journey at PAF-IAST

Merit-based admissions open to all qualified students. Need-based and merit scholarships available.

How to Apply

1

Check Eligibility

FSc Pre-Engineering/Pre-Medical with minimum 60% marks (or equivalent A-Levels). NTS/Entry test required for most programs.

2

Submit Online Application

Fill in the application form at paf-iast.edu.pk with all academic documents, CNIC/B-Form, and passport-size photo.

3

Entry Test / Interview

Appear for the university's entry test on the scheduled date. Shortlisted candidates may be called for an interview.

4

Merit List & Enrollment

Merit lists are announced online. Confirm your seat by depositing the fee within the deadline to secure enrollment.
